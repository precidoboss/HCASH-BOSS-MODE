🆓 FREE MODE FREE
A Grotto Miner NFT image appears on the pedestal. Three stone pillars show possible names — pick the correct one before time runs out.

10 rounds · 3 lives — each wrong answer or timeout costs a life. Difficulty scales with each round: Easy → Medium → Hard → Extreme.

Build answer streaks for up to ×5 score multipliers. You get one ⛏ HINT per game — it eliminates a wrong option but costs 30 points. Reach round 10 alive to master the grotto.

💀 GROTTO TRIALS PAID · WIN TOKENS
Pay $1 worth of tokens to unlock 3 chances. No entry limit — buy as many chances as you need to beat the leaderboard.

You get a single 60-second window to identify as many miners as possible. No per-question timer — work at your own pace, but every second counts.

Options are deliberately confusing — similar names, same categories. Your score from that 60-second run is submitted to the leaderboard. Only your best run counts.
